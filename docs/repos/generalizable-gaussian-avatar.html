Generalizable Gaussian Avatar

Research codebase for GAGAvatar-style dynamic Gaussian head avatars.

Training on tracked video data, inference with a reference identity and driver frames, and optional fine-tuning or combined training paths. Built around Lightning Fabric training, FLAME mesh helpers, and Gaussian rendering utilities.

Highlights

  • Configs for model, data, FLAME, and camera settings
  • Training (train.py), inference (inference.py), combined and fine-tuning entry points
  • Face tracking / matting via GAGAvatar_track submodule
  • Video-to-video style inference (vid2vid_infer.py)

Tech

Python · PyTorch · 3D Gaussian avatars · FLAME · TensorBoard
