Multi-Language Lip-Sync (VisualDub)

Generative AI system for cross-lingual audio-to-lip motion synthesis with realistic facial animation.

---

🚀 Problem

Generating realistic lip movements for arbitrary audio across different languages is challenging due to variations in phonemes, timing, and facial dynamics. Maintaining identity consistency and temporal coherence is critical for production use.

---

💡 Solution

Built a generative model that maps audio signals to 3D facial keypoints and drives motion-based rendering for realistic, identity-preserving lip-sync across languages.

---

🏗️ System Design

Input Audio
   ↓
Audio Feature Extraction (Mel Spectrogram)
   ↓
Audio Encoder
   ↓
3D Keypoint Prediction
   ↓
Motion Estimation Network
   ↓
Warping / Rendering Network
   ↓
Final Video Frames
---

⚙️ Key Components

1. Audio Processing

  • Mel-spectrogram extraction
  • Temporal audio feature encoding

2. 3D Keypoint Modeling

  • Implicit facial keypoint extraction
  • Head pose and expression modeling

3. Motion Estimation

  • Dense motion fields
  • Keypoint-based deformation

4. Warping & Rendering

  • Feature warping using motion fields
  • SPADE-based or GAN-based decoding

5. Identity Preservation

  • Maintains facial identity across frames
  • Ensures consistency under motion and expression changes
---

🧠 Techniques Used
